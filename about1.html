About Us

We started in 2015 as a way to pay for bills; now as the world seems to be an endless spiral of bad news we're back for "good." Volunteering and donating time is a passion for us so we decided to combine them.

Good Deed Ideas So Far

Buy a family's groceries at the store, Distribute gift cards (starbucks, walmart, amazon), Pay for the people behind us all day, Donate to suggested charities/gofundmes, Send students from low-income areas to Prom in a limo, Buy out a street vendor, Send flowers to a retirement community
